About Me

Over the last ten years, I took over 9000 appointments as a hairdresser/stylist/guru/artist. I worked alongside and with over 40 other uniquely talented individuals. I excelled in wedding hair, detailed and picture-perfect hair color transformations and even barbering. Technology radpidly changed my potential. From quality user-friendly appointment booking software, my own social media posts and to Instagram balayage tutorials: I was getting appointments, marketing my business, and learning trade secrets that were incredibly valuble.

During those years, I moved away from my hometown of Champaign, Illinois and settled in Denver. I met my husband. I started a mildly extreme gym experience and after 3 years, sustained a mildly extreme injury. That made my work suddenly intollerable and I started dreaming up a new plan. I never intended to stay in the beauty industry forever, so it was all for the best.

Web developement was an easy choice, for all I knew about it. I didn't really know anything at the time I made the decision, but once I decide something, my path is clear. I've been discovering so much potential for coding in my life, and potential in myself for the code.
